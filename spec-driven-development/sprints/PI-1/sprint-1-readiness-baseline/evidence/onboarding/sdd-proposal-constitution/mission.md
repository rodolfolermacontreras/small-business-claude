# Mission

Owner: Rodolfo Lerma

This proposal was extracted from repository archaeology for `Small-Business-Claude` at `C:\Training\Projects\Small-Business-Claude`.

Small-Business-Claude is a local, runnable AI copilot for small business owners. It runs
on the owner's own machine, powered by the Claude API, with a friendly web UI. It offers
6 ready-to-run workflows (chase overdue invoices, plan payroll, close the month, business
pulse, campaign planner, lead triage) and a human-in-the-loop approval gate: anything that
would send, post, or pay is drafted and queued in the "Needs your approval" panel until
the owner clicks Approve. A connector layer (QuickBooks / PayPal / HubSpot) uses mock data
today, shaped to mirror real API responses so it can swap to live OAuth calls later without
changing the rest of the app.
